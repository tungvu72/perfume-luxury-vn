# Format Mix

## Recommended 10-video mix
- 5 ranking videos
- 2 battle videos
- 2 problem-solving videos
- 1 opinion/identity video

## Why
- Ranking pulls reach
- Battle pulls comments
- Problem-solving pulls trust and purchase intent
- Opinion builds channel voice and loyalty

## Practical publish rhythm
Use a cadence like:
- ranking
- practical
- battle
- ranking
- pain-point/problem-solving
- practical luxury/aspirational
- battle or identity
- ranking

## Fatigue warning signs
Reduce ranking share if:
- CTR falls while topic quality remains broad
- comments start saying videos feel repetitive
- browse traffic weakens across similar uploads
- recognizable bottles repeat too often in back-to-back uploads
