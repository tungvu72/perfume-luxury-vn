# Audience Model

## Current core audience assumptions
Use these unless fresher channel evidence overrides them.

- Audience is overwhelmingly male
- Core age band is roughly 35–54
- Mobile is primary, TV usage is also meaningful
- Broad, mature, practical topics outperform niche or trend-chasing topics

## What usually fits this audience
- clear titles
- familiar bottles
- mature masculinity
- practical buying/use-case framing
- authority or social-proof framing

## What usually underperforms
- women-focused pivots
- niche note-specific topics as mass content
- negative list formats like “most hated”
- overly edgy or ironic packaging

## Implications
- Thumbnail text should be short and readable from far away
- Topic should be understandable in 1 second
- Ranking tension and social proof are strong levers
- Identity and respect/status framing can work well when not overdone
